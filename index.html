PROJECTS

My Projects

Filipino Deepfake Audio Detection Tool Using Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Transformer Models

2025
With deepfake audio becoming more prevalent and its potential misuse in fraud, misinformation, and identity theft, we developed the first-ever Filipino deepfake audio detection tool for our thesis project. Our approach combined deep learning models (ResNet-50, VGG-16), transformer models (Wav2Vec2, HuBERT), and machine learning models (Decision Tree, Extra Trees, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting). We trained these models on our own Filipino audio dataset to classify deepfake and genuine audio in the Filipino language. The best-performing models (ResNet-50, VGG-16, HuBERT, and Wav2Vec2) all achieved an impressive 100% accuracy in classification. Through this project, I gained in-depth knowledge of deep learning, machine learning models, and transformer-based architectures, and integrating them into web applications.

Scan and Sort: Mobile Application for Household Waste Classification Using ResNet-50

2024
Scan and Sort is a mobile application for household waste classification. My team and I trained a ResNet-50 model on our own dataset of 15,000 images across 15 household waste categories. The model achieved an impressive 93.88% training accuracy and 90.33% validation accuracy. Through this project, I deepened my understanding of training and fine-tuning deep learning models, dataset preparation, and model evaluation, as well as deploying them in mobile applications to address real-world problems such as efficient waste management.

TranslatePH: Filipino-English Translation Mobile Application

2023
Developed a mobile application that facilitates translation between Filipino and English using text and voice input. It utilizes Google APIs for Text-to-Speech (TTS) and Speech-to-Text (STT) for speech recognition and translation. The app also features Firebase Authentication for secure login and a currency converter with real-time exchange rates to Philippine Pesos (PHP), making communication more accessible for users looking to learn both English and Filipino.
